About

Dr. Neena Khanna

Dr. Neena Khanna stands as a distinguished figure in the realm of dermatology, commanding reverence and admiration as the Head of the Department of Dermatology at Amrita Hospital, Faridabad. With a sterling career that spans over four decades, she has become a paragon of excellence in clinical research and teaching. Dr. Khanna's journey began with the pursuit of medical knowledge at Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore, where she earned her MBBS. Subsequently, she further honed her expertise by attaining an MD in Dermatology from the esteemed All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi.

Dr. Khanna's multifaceted proficiency encompasses various facets of dermatology, including aesthetic dermatology, pediatric dermatology, and specialized areas such as acne, psoriasis, vitiligo, autoimmune blistering diseases, PCOS, leprosy, and other pigmentary disorders. Her commitment to advancing medical knowledge is underscored by her extensive experience in both clinical practice and academic pursuits.

A stalwart in her field, Dr. Neena Khanna epitomizes the synergy between theoretical knowledge and practical application, contributing significantly to the advancement of dermatological science. Her legacy is not merely defined by her impressive credentials, but also by the countless lives she has positively impacted through her compassionate care and unwavering dedication to the field of dermatology.
